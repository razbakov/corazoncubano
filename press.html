if you're writing about us

Four angles, four stories.

Not every newsroom needs the same hook. Here are four ways into the Corazón Cubano story — pick the one that fits your beat.

01

The other Munich week

Munich's global brand is Oktoberfest — a single week of beer-tent culture. Corazón Cubano is building a counter-week the first week of July: Cuban music, dance, food. The story of a city earning a second identity.

02

A refuge that travels with you

Refuge for culture, not asylum for people. What does it mean to give Cuban music, language and dance a year-round home in a Bavarian city — and why 5% of everything goes back to the island it came from.

03

Los Van Van in München

The Cuban orchestra that defined timba is headlining Edition 1, Saturday 3 July 2027 at Westin Grand. For music critics: what it means to bring an orchestra with a 55-year arc to a German cultural festival, and what comes after on the program.

04

Eleven friends and a Verein

The festival is run by an 11-person founding circle organising as a German e.V. (nonprofit registered association). Not a corporation. Not a touring promoter. For business-of-culture writers: how a German nonprofit structure shapes what kind of festival this becomes.
